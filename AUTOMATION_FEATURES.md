# NoxTitan™ Automation, Customization, and Compliance Features

## Legal & Compliance Notice
All features are designed to comply with HIPAA, labor laws, and privacy regulations. Users are responsible for configuring automations and templates in accordance with their organization’s legal requirements. NoxTitan™ provides tools for audit logging, access control, and data encryption. For full compliance documentation, contact legal@noxtitan.com.

## Built-in Automations
- Shift scheduling (auto-assign, conflict resolution)
- Time-off approvals and notifications
- Payroll calculations and export
- Compliance alerts (labor law, overtime, certifications)
- Onboarding workflows (documents, training, e-signatures)
- Employee recognition and rewards
- Performance review reminders
- Incident reporting and escalation
- Facility maintenance scheduling
- Visitor management (badges, watchlists)
- Inventory and asset tracking
- Custom report generation (filter, export, visualize)
- Automated analytics dashboards (KPIs, trends, forecasts)
- Email/SMS/push notifications for any event
- Data backup and audit logging
- Policy review and acknowledgment tracking
- SOP update notifications
- Document expiration and renewal reminders

## User-Created Automations
- Drag-and-drop automation builder (triggers, actions, conditions)

- Integration with external APIs (HRIS, payroll, calendar, messaging)
